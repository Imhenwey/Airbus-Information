More information on the A220 Family

The Family

The A220 Family is the newest member of the Airbus family. It is designed for the 100-150 seat market and offers a range of features that make it a great choice for airlines and passengers alike. The A220 is known for its fuel efficiency, comfort, and advanced technology.

The Family includes the A220-100 and A220-300 models, which are designed to meet the needs of airlines operating in the regional and short-haul markets. The A220 is equipped with the latest technology, including advanced aerodynamics, lightweight materials, and state-of-the-art engines.

A220-100

The A220-100 is the smaller member of the A220 Family, designed for regional and short-haul operations. It can seat up to 120 passengers in a two-class configuration and offers a range of up to 3,400 nautical miles.

It is powered by Pratt & Whitney's PW1500G geared turbofan engines, which provide excellent fuel efficiency and reduced noise levels. The aircraft features a spacious cabin with larger windows, wider seats, and improved overhead storage.

A220-300

The A220-300 is the larger member of the A220 Family, designed for higher capacity operations. It can seat up to 160 passengers in a two-class configuration and offers a range of up to 3,400 nautical miles.

Like the A220-100, it is powered by Pratt & Whitney's PW1500G geared turbofan engines, providing excellent fuel efficiency and reduced noise levels. The aircraft features a spacious cabin with larger windows, wider seats, and improved overhead storage.
